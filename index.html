My Blog

Exploring Car Sales with Power BI

This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of car sales performance. It features informative cards showing:

  • Average COGS Cost
  • Average Labor Cost
  • Average Shipping Cost
  • Average Sales Price
  • Number of Dealers
  • Number of Customers
  • Count of Credit-Worthy Buyers
  • Most Affordable & Expensive Car Prices

Users can interact with slicers to filter by Make, Country, and Year.

Additional visualizations include:

  • Color Preferences (Pie Chart)
  • Car Sales Trend by Month

The dashboard also supports toggles for Calendar Year (CY) and Fiscal Year (FY), as well as options to switch between Count and Percent View—a method I use regularly for building dashboards for CJA and MOCJ NYC.
